**Gaming Grid** is a collaborative community for people passionate about gaming and game creation.

We bring together gamers and creators across multiple disciplines, including:

- Gamers  
- Indie game developers  
- Streamers and YouTubers  
- Designers and digital artists  
- Animators, VFX artists, and video editors  

Our primary focus is gaming, with a strong emphasis on creators who actively contribute content, tools, or experiences to the gaming ecosystem. Whether you create games, stream content, or support creative production, Gaming Grid provides a space to connect, collaborate, and grow.

Our mission is to build a sustainable and supportive environment where creativity and collaboration thrive.

- Support indie developers through feedback and playtesting  
- Encourage collaboration over competition  
- Help creators grow skills, projects, and audiences  
- Share knowledge, experience, and resources  
- Promote creativity, respect, and teamwork
